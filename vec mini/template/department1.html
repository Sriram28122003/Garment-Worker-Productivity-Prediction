Departments

Cutting Department

Responsible for cutting fabrics according to the patterns provided by the design department. Ensures minimal wastage of fabric and accuracy in cuts.

Design Department

Responsible for creating garment designs and ensuring they meet market trends and consumer preferences. Provides technical drawings and specifications to guide production.

Sewing Department

Assembles the cut pieces into finished garments. Ensures stitching quality and efficiency in the sewing process. Often divided into lines or cells for specific operations.

Finishing Department

Handles the final steps of garment production, including trimming, ironing, and packing. Ensures that finished garments meet quality standards before shipping
